Get the Mobile App Version

Access the archive instantly right from your smartphone's home screen with seamless, beautiful borders.

First, open this link on your mobile phone browser:

https://jsg-archive.vercel.app/

Android Instructions (Opera, Brave, Chrome)

  1. Open your preferred mobile browser and go to the link above.
  2. Tap the browser's Menu button (usually three dots near the top or bottom right corner).
  3. Select "Install App" or "Add to Home screen" from the list.
  4. Confirm the prompt, and a standalone shortcut icon will be instantly placed on your phone's desktop!

iPhone & iOS Instructions (Safari)

  1. Open Safari on your iPhone and navigate to the link above.
  2. Tap the Share button at the bottom of the screen (the square icon with an arrow pointing up).
  3. Scroll down through the share options menu and select "Add to Home Screen".
  4. Tap Add in the top right corner, and the archive app wrapper is ready on your screen!
