Dr. John Stafford Geddes Archive
10 June 1939 – 9 October 2024
Pioneer of the Belfast "Flying Squad" & Portable Defibrillator • Visionary Cardiologist
Northern Irish cardiologist who co-developed the world's first mobile coronary care unit (Belfast's "Flying Squad") in 1966, enabling portable defibrillation and revolutionizing pre-hospital cardiac care. His visionary influence extended globally, notably pioneering the implementation of defibrillators on commercial airlines including Qantas.
